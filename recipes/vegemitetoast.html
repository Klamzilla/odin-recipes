Vegemite Toast

The Greatest Australian breakfast!

Nothing is more Australian than vegemite on toast for breakfast before the day starts!

The creamy butter, the crunch and the distinct vegemite flavour have been raising Australians for generations.

Ingredients
  • Your choice of Bread
  • Vegemite
  • Softened Butter
Steps
  1. Toast bread until golden brown, or your own desired colour.
  2. Spread this layer of softened butter onto bread.
  3. Once the butter has almost melted, spread your desired amount of vegemite.
  4. Enjoy!
