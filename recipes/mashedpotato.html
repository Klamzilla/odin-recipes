Mashed Potato

A classic Staple!

We all know and love this recipe!

The secret to good mashed potato are the potatoes you use, Desirees are best!

Ingredients
  • 500 grams of potatoes
  • 50 grams of butter
  • 100 millilitres of milk
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
Steps
  1. Steam or Boil potatoes until knife can penetrate with no resistance.
  2. Heat milk and butter.
  3. Mash potatoes using mashing device.
  4. Add milk, butter, salt and pepper to taste and mix.
